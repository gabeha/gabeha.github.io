photos

4 years ago, I came back from my travels and bought a camera. I got a LUMIX G70 with the kit lens and a tripod. Pretty soon, I realized I wanted a bit more versatility and got a 1.7 24mm portrait lens. I started editing the photos on my phone until I saved up enough to buy a Laptop - that was in early 2019.

From that point on, I took photos on every occasion - I got particularly fascinated by Fan Ho's street photography. So I started roaming the streets of my home town, Essen. During that time, I worked as a waiter in a Burger restaurant which allowed me to take long weekends off and travel around Europe. Many of my favourite pictures come from those trips.

PART OF ME

"PART OF ME" is a film portraying the journey of an athlete. From his first steps on the Rugby pitch to his present day gym career, Akiya's love for exercise has governed his life. He talks about the reasons why he embraces going through pain and suffering and what it means to him as a person.

RO-BOTANY

With the rising demand for plant crops, there is a need for technologies that can help to meet the demand, as well as improve the quality and quantity of the products supplied. The aim of this project was to develop an automated plant box as a potential solution to optimize the production of plant crops. It was made to monitor conditions such as humidity, temperature, and soil moisture percentage, adjusting them using a Raspberry Pi and several Arduinos.
